There are millions of people who are unable to obtain approval for signature loans and other financial services from the traditional banking system. As a result, these individuals often turn to alternative sources to meet their funding needs. However, they cannot build a reliable credit history with these loans, as their credit transactions are not officially recorded, leading to a vicious circle.

The solution is to build a system as they would, with a traditional bank loan that stores credit records objectively.
